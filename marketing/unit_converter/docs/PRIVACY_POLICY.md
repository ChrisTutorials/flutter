# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 11, 2026  
**App:** Unit Converter  
**Version:** 1.0.0

## Introduction

This privacy policy explains how the Unit Converter app ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your information. The App is designed to provide unit conversion functionality with minimal data collection.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information:** Operating system version, device model (for ad compatibility)
- **Usage Data:** Number of conversions performed, ad interactions (anonymous)
- **Network Information:** IP address (temporary, for currency exchange rates)

### 2. Information You Provide
- **Custom Units:** Unit names and conversion ratios you create (stored locally only)
- **App Preferences:** Theme settings, favorite conversions (stored locally only)

### 3. Third-Party Services
- **Google AdMob:** Collects device data for ad personalization
- **Currency API:** Fetches exchange rates from frankfurter.dev

## How We Use Your Information

- **To Provide Services:** Currency conversions, custom unit storage
- **To Improve the App:** Anonymous usage statistics for feature improvements
- **To Support the App:** Displaying relevant advertisements
- **To Ensure Compatibility:** Device information for proper functioning

## Data Storage & Security

- **Local Storage:** All personal data (custom units, preferences) is stored only on your device
- **No Account Required:** The App does not collect personal identifiers like email or name
- **No Data Sharing:** We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information
- **Encryption:** Network requests use standard HTTPS encryption

## Third-Party Services

### Google AdMob
The App uses Google AdMob for displaying advertisements. Google may collect:
- Device identifiers
- Approximate location
- Usage patterns

[Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### Currency Exchange API
The App uses frankfurter.dev for currency exchange rates:
- Requests are made anonymously
- No personal data is transmitted
- Service provides public financial data

## Your Rights

- **Access:** You can view all data stored in the app
- **Deletion:** Clear app data through device settings
- **Opt-out:** Disable ads through premium purchase
- **Correction:** Edit or delete custom units anytime

## Children's Privacy

The App does not:
- Collect personal information from children under 13
- Target children in advertising
- Require age verification

## Data Retention

- **Local Data:** Retained until you delete the app or clear app data
- **Analytics Data:** Retained anonymously for service improvement
- **Ad Data:** Managed by Google AdMob according to their policies

## International Users

Your data may be processed in countries where our service providers operate. We take appropriate measures to protect your information in accordance with applicable laws.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting in the app and on our website.
