Unit Converter is a fast, polished measurement tool for everyday calculations, travel, study, and field work.

Convert across core measurement categories, check live currency rates, save favorite conversions, and build your own custom units for workflows that standard apps do not cover.

Key features:
- Fast unit conversion across length, weight, temperature, volume, area, cooking, speed, data, pressure, and time
- Live currency conversion with a dedicated currency screen
- Custom units for your own formulas and reference measurements
- Favorites and recent history for repeat conversions
- Real-world comparison hints for selected conversions
- Home screen widget support
- Light and dark themes with multiple palettes
- Offline-friendly experience for standard conversions

Built for students, engineers, makers, travelers, cooks, and anyone who needs accurate conversions without friction.

The app includes ads, with a one-time upgrade available to remove them.
